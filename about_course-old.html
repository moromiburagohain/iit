About the Course

The Applied Machine Learning & Data Science Certification by E&ICT Academy, IIT Guwahati, is a comprehensive program designed to bridge academic learning with industry applications in AI and ML.

Why Choose This Course?

  • Hands-on Focus: Learn to build, deploy, and manage real-world AI/ML solutions.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Covers Python, Deep Learning, NLP, and Cloud ML tools.
  • Expert-Led: Delivered by IIT faculty and industry professionals.
  • Career Support: Includes resume-building, mock interviews, and career counseling.
  • Portfolio Development: Capstone project to showcase your expertise.
