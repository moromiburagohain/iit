History

The E&ICT Academy at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati) was established as a major initiative to enhance capacity building in the critical areas of Electronics and Information & Communication Technology across the North Eastern Region of India and beyond.

Date Event Significance
March 26, 2015 Project Commencement Project launched under the scheme Financial Assistance for setting up Electronics and ICT Academies by MeitY.
January 19, 2016 Formal Inauguration The Academy was inaugurated by the Honble Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi.
Past 10 Years Training Volume Successfully conducted 400+ programs through classroom and online modes.
